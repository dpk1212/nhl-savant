## NORTH STAR
The mission is **1,000,000 followers**. Every post is judged by one question: *would a stranger screenshot or repost this?* Reach is fine (1.2K–1.9K views/post); the bottleneck is **shareability** — we have ~0 retweets. Fix that and growth compounds. Always think one move ahead: *what is the single best NEXT post for virality, right now?*

## Current viral hook shapes in our lane
- **Sharp-vs-public market read (TOP format)** — *@invisiblestats* "Public vs Sharps, [round]" hit **70K engagement**; *@PatrickE_Vegas* runs the same shape. Line 1 = the split or the money; proof = side-by-side $/% (+ line move); close = a verdict. This is the niche's highest-engagement structure and the one we keep under-using. The most viral version is a **clean-sweep / "$0 on the other side"** read — the lopsidedness IS the hook.
- **Obscure-game / underdog whale** — six-figure (or one-sided) sharp money on a game/side the public ignores. Curiosity gap + "we see what you don't" + whale tape. Our likeliest first retweet.
- **"N bets the sharps like" authority list** — *@WizBetz* "MLB Play of the Day", *@invisiblestats* — clean, scannable list. Maps to our board if we open with a read, not a setup line.
- **Honest record flex / recap** — post the units AND the Ls (e.g. "+29u since June 1, 158-130, we post every L"). Honesty is our rarest edge and a strong identity beat.

## Tweet-construction tricks landing now
- **The contrast/twist opener** — two short lines that flip ("$29K on one side. $0 on the other."). The gap between the two lines is the screenshot. Most reposted shape we can build.
- **Growth-creator lane runs LONG & thread-shaped** — kept corpus skews thread-shaped, ~2K+ chars, ~half use emoji (@AlexFinn's "I read all 24,000 lines of the X algo, here's everything 🧵" = 30K eng). When a play has depth, THREAD it (hook + reply) instead of a 6-line wall.
- **Volume + real voice** — @rachcorrine (15K eng): tweet more than feels comfortable, have a real opinion, be a real person, build community via replies. Cadence is itself a lever.
- **Reach is throttled, not removed** (@GiveMeBanHammer, 36K eng): low-signal/over-posting gets deboosted; dense high-signal posts get carried. Make every post earn its slot.
- **First line must be a standalone market read**, not a pick list — that's the reposted line.

## TIMING — sharp positions move, so freshness sells (NEW standing rule)
- **≤ 60 min to game = firm.** Hero these with a countdown ("first pitch in ~30 min") — the tape is effectively locked and the urgency drives action.
- **> 60 min = still hero-able if it's the most viral read, but ALWAYS flag it can move** ("~2h out, money's still coming in"). Never imply a far-out number is final.
- Name the game time (ET) every post. Re-pull the board if it's >30 min stale before posting.

## Growth levers
- Lead with the market split or the money, prove with the tape (≤3 slots), close with a binary side choice.
- One quotable standalone line per post — this is our #1 fix (zero-RT gap).
- Reply to your own hero within ~30 min with one extra data point (name the lead wallet's rank/ROI; or a second game the same whale is on). Dwell + early engagement.
- Rotate identity/contrarian/record beats so the feed stays human, not a wall of cards.
- Thread deep reads instead of truncating.

## Stale / stop
- Setup-line / pick-list openers with no market read ("Massive slate tonight" → ~1 like).
- Soft "thoughts?" closes — use a binary side choice.
- 6+ proof-line walls — thread or trim to 3.
- Quoting far-out positions as if locked, or quoting settled/in-progress totals as tailable.
