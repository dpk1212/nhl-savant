## Current viral hook shapes in our lane
- **Whale tape** — "A wallet up +$Xk just bet [N]× its normal size." Pure voyeurism + FOMO. Our top labeled format.
- **Sharp vs public split** — "Public money: X% on [team]. Sharp money: Y% on [other]." Niche-validated market-map opener; the pick reads as the verdict.
- **Honest recap / bounce-back** — own the L, then pivot to where the money's going next. Builds trust, doesn't bleed momentum.
- **CLV flex** — "We locked it at [price]; it's now [price] — a +X% line beat before kickoff." Credible to sharps.

## Tweet construction tricks (landing now)
- First 10 words must carry the topic + the tension (sport/slate + money split) — for the algorithm and for humans.
- Compact proof: line-1 hook, then ≤3 bullets (public % · sharp $ · move/wallet). Extra detail goes to replies.
- Build save-worthy "boards" (Public / Sharp / Move / Verdict) people screenshot and bookmark.
- Close with a real binary side choice, not "thoughts?".

## Growth levers
- Reply to your own post within the first ~30 min with one extra data point (dwell + early engagement).
- Rotate identity/contrarian beats so the feed stays human, not a wall of cards.
- One quotable standalone line per post to finally earn reposts (our biggest gap).
