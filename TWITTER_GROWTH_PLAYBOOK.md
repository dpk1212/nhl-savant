## Tweet-construction tricks landing now (from the cleaned growth corpus)
- **Bold-claim + "here's everything you need to know 🧵" opener** — *@AlexFinn* ("Elon just updated the entire X algorithm code / I went through all 24,000 lines / here's everything you need to know about how to go viral 🧵", 30K eng). The opener makes a big, specific claim then promises the payoff. Maps to: "$207K just hit a game nobody's watching. Here's who's loading it 👇".
- **Growth-creator lane runs LONG and thread-shaped** — kept corpus: **80% thread-shaped, avg ~2,288 chars, 51% emoji.** Threads/long-form out-reach quippy one-liners with these accounts. For us: when a single play has a deep tape, thread it (hook tweet + reply with the wallet detail) instead of cramming 6 proof lines.
- **Volume + voice beats polish** — *@rachcorrine* (15K eng) growth checklist: tweet more than feels comfortable (timeline moves fast), have a real opinion, show you're a real person, reply and build community, keep 2-5 focus topics (not one rigid niche). Cadence is itself a growth lever.
- **Reach is throttled, not removed** — *@GiveMeBanHammer* (36K eng) on the 2026 algo: low-signal/over-posting gets deboosted, "high-quality" dense posts get carried. Translation: make every post earn its slot — one clean read, real numbers, a quotable line. Don't spray low-effort cards.
- **First line must be a standalone market read**, not a pick list — that's the reposted line.

## Growth levers
- Lead with the market split or the money, prove with the tape (≤3 slots), close with a binary side choice.
- One quotable standalone line per post (our zero-RT gap — still our #1 fix).
- Reply to your own post within ~30 min with one extra data point (name the lead wallet, its rank/ROI). Dwell + early engagement.
- Rotate identity/contrarian beats so the feed stays human, not a wall of cards.
- When a play has depth, **thread it** rather than truncating — the lane rewards long, structured proof.

## Stale / stop
- Short pick-list openers with no market read ("Massive slate tonight" → 3 likes today is the proof).
- Over-relying on "thoughts?"-style soft closes — use a binary side choice.
- Quoting six-figure totals that are already settled/in-progress as if they're tailable. Verify the kickoff time every run.
