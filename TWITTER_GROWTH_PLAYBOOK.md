## Current viral hook shapes in our lane
- **Obscure-game whale (NEW, top growth pick)** — six-figure sharp money on a game the public isn't watching (e.g. $206K on Uzbekistan vs DR Congo). Curiosity gap + status ("we see what you don't") + whale tape in one line. Most quotable hook we have — our likeliest first retweet. Line 1 = "Sharps just dropped $X on a game you've never heard of."
- **Sharp-vs-public board** — *@PatrickE_Vegas* (103K engagement on one post). Line 1 names the game + promises the sharp-vs-public split; proof is a side-by-side (public % vs sharp $ + line move); close is a verdict on which side the market is wrong about. **This is the single highest-engagement structure in our niche and we rarely post it.**
- **Honest recap board** — *@AlexCaruso* "MLB Recap:" — label the results instantly, stack wins/misses with numbers, close with a lesson.
- **Whale tape** — a single wallet's outsized bet (our own top-performing original format).
- **Public vs Sharps, [round]** — *@invisiblestats* — clean contrarian framing.

## Tweet-construction tricks landing now
- **In-lane niche posts run LONG** — avg ~1,141 chars, **77% use emoji**, **89% use numbers**, only **11% ask a question**. Translation: dense, emoji-tagged, number-heavy boards beat short quippy posts in our lane. (We over-use questions and under-use length/emoji.)
- **Contrarian list hooks** (from growth_tips): *@nicktheriot_* "Amateur 'research': → … Real research: → …" — the "wrong way vs right way" list converts. Maps to our "public bets the logo / sharps bet the number" framing.
- **First line must be a standalone market read**, not a pick list — that's the reposted line.

## Growth levers
- Lead with the market split or the money, prove with the tape, close with a binary side choice.
- One quotable standalone line per post (our zero-RT gap).
- Reply to your own post within ~30 min with one extra data point (dwell + early engagement).
- Rotate identity/contrarian beats so the feed stays human, not a wall of cards.
