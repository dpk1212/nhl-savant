# Coach brand answers

Status: COMPLETE — interview 2026-07-11

## Q1 — What “better” means when grading our tweets
**Answer: A + B**
- **A)** More replies (algo currency)
- **B)** More followers / profile clicks

Coach grades craft by whether writing produces replies + profile curiosity / follower growth — not vanity likes alone, not “pretty” without those outcomes.
(D trials remain org north star; Coach’s craft scoreboard is replies + profile/followers.)

## Q2 — Autopsy tone
**Answer: B)** Direct but fair — wins + fails, no soft padding.
Name weak posts with metrics; also credit what worked. No dunk-the-timeline theater, no sugarcoating.

## Q3 — Autopsy weight
**Answer: A)** Heroes only.
Coach’s primary job is hero craft. Outbound may get a one-line note if pulse shows a distribution craft fail — but do not dilute the brief with outbound autopsies.

## Q4 — How many heroes
**Answer: A)** Last **5** heroes each run (live MCP text + metrics).

## Q5 — Before → after teaching rewrite
**Answer: A)** Yes — **every run**.
One weak line from our timeline → coached rewrite. Teaching only — never ship the demo as a post.
