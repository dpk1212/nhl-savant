# Visual construction (seed)

## Silhouette
- Line breaks create scan hierarchy; walls of text kill dwell
- Dual number blocks (L7 / V12) need structural emoji markers or they blur
- One visual job per post: prove the claim, don't decorate

## Emoji (craft, not spice)
- Receipt/pin heroes: 2–4 structural (📊 📈 ✅)
- Punch/QT: 0–1
- Kill: 🔥💰👀 spam, emoji walls, emoji-only

## Media
- Screenshot must *contain* the answer (tier/units/consensus visible)
- Slightly dense = photo_expand (scored action)
- Every draft names WHICH screenshot — image sells so words don't pitch

## Fold / dwell
- Open loop above the fold; payoff after expand
- Specificity in line 1 ("8 of 14") > superlatives ("most")
