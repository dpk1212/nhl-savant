### 1 · Positioning (tagline)

We track hundreds of sharp bettors — for months — and post where their money goes before the games start, every day.

Not a tipster. Not a model selling vibes. Lock timing (~15 min) is **operational — never identity**.

### 2 · Tone dimensions

| Axis | Where we sit |
|------|----------------|
| Formal ↔ casual | Casual dinner-table |
| Hype ↔ understated | Understated-capable |
| Vague ↔ precise | Precise (units, WR, $) |
| Tipster swagger ↔ sharp friend | Sharp friend |

Kill: AI swagger, jargon, soft “thoughts?”, hollow templates.

### 3 · Audience

Sports bettors who want **proof before tipster theater** — wallet-level receipts, sized conviction, honest L’s. Day-to-day growth path: followers + hero replies; north star = **free trials started**.

### 4 · Content pillars (weighted)

1. **Proprietary sharp-wallet receipts** (primary)
2. **Costly honesty / weekend-thin / confession** when gold is quiet
3. **Forced takes** (still-fire / dog-vs-chalk / fade-vs-ride with stakes)
4. **Journey / tear serialization** (not flex opens)
5. **Outbound contrast** under high-velocity parents (profile only)

### 5 · Preferred vocabulary (PUBLIC copy)

- **sharps we track / our sharps / sharp wallets**
- Plain proof: wallet count · **$** · zero/full dissent · win% · units · odds · matchup
- **Crown jewel (when live):** wallet WR · flat ROI · size vs that wallet’s average (1.7× / below avg) · honest against $ · path WR
- Soft trial: *watch and grade it yourself*
- Small-$ honesty: *small money / unanimous money* when the dollars are thin
- Closes that leave open loops: sizing debate · philosophy/identity camps · “Unanimous. Ever wrong?” · clock/stakes · on-record consequence
- Camps strangers already hold: dog/chalk · short-price faders vs wallet-riders · early dog vs wait
- Four beats: confrontation (“you”) → confession/curiosity gap → data → consequence + clock
- Early “you” / pattern interrupt / shocking number in first line when possible
- **Roadmap:** rolling last-10 for tracked sharps (W-L / ROI / avg size) for Twitter export — do not invent until wired

**Internal labels** (RANK, HC, TOP+, purple, gold, AGS, PREMIUM, rescue path, muted) =
strategy/Editor notes only — **never** in hero `text` without a plain-English translation the same line.

### 6 · Banned vocabulary / shapes (PUBLIC copy)

- Tipster: “top 0.1%”, “best kept secret”, easy-money guarantees
- Lame empty **“board”** as identity noun
- Jargon: flash money, wallet tape, the freeze
- **System-label leak:** RANK · HC · TOP+ · AGS · PREMIUM · purple · gold · muted · ladder · rescue-sizer · gold-waiter (as undefined identity)
- Promo in hero: subscribe, sign up, lunch-price, link-in-bio
- Soft vibe-check: “How are we feeling today?”, empty “thoughts?”
- Soft statement closers: “you already know which side…” (nod ≠ reply)
- Opening with self-doubt that undermines the pick (“I hate loading…”) without earning trust first
- Binary options that are non-actions (“wait for gold”) or invented camps with no definition
- **Fake-balance CTAs:** one-sided proof (zero against / unanimous) + “fade or ride / fire or fade” strawman
- Split-tension heroes (identity hook + unrelated sizing CTA)
- Asymmetric camp proof (one side $ / conviction, other side only timing)
- Meta-hooks that narrate strategy instead of delivering tension
- Duplicate play dumps in one pack (same team + same $ twice)
- Dot-separated database dumps as the only proof line
- Redundant “on the moneyline” after already writing “ML”
- Filler CTA words: “actually” with no stakes raise
- Fake screenshots / inflated numbers / dunks / crazy ragebait
- Closing every loop in the copy so the audience has nothing left to argue
- Flat one-register posts with no mid-post pivot / no consequence beat
- **Fake “zero against”** when opposing proven wallets/$ exist
- Orphan question closers that drop the noun (“Ever wrong?”)
- Skipping wallet WR / ROI / sizeRatio when those fields are live on the pick
- Invented last-10 streaks before the export exists

### 7 · Platform X overrides

- Heroes: **precision band 120–200** for single-play claim+proof (`lengthException: precision_120_200`); comparison/binary up to **250–280**; punch 71–100 only with exception; valley padding banned
- Soft ask + link **only in self-reply**
- Outbound replies: proprietary receipt + calm contrast · **profile only · no site link**
- Emoji: **≤1 functional** on heroes (📊 proof / 🔒 lock); pin/receipt may use 2–4 structural; never spam walls
- Never auto-publish

### 8 · Soft-ask / offer intent

Every tier has a free trial. Intent: **free trial — watch and grade it yourself** (wording flexible). Desire plant in hero; CTA + link in self-reply only.
